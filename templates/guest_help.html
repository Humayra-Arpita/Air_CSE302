Guest Help Centre

How to Book a Stay

To book a stay, start by entering your destination, travel dates, and number of guests in the search bar. Use filters to narrow down your preferences such as price range, property type, and amenities. Once you've found a listing you like, review the details, host policies, and guest reviews. Click “Reserve” or “Request to Book,” then follow the prompts to complete your payment securely. A confirmation will be sent once your booking is approved.

Account & Payments

To manage your account, go to your profile to update personal information, contact details, and payment preferences. Airbnb supports multiple secure payment methods, including credit/debit cards and digital wallets. You’ll be charged once a booking is confirmed, and receipts are available in your account. For cancellations, applicable refunds are processed automatically according to the host's cancellation policy. Always keep your payment details up to date to avoid booking issues.
