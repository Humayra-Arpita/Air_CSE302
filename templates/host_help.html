Host Help Centre

How to List Your Property

To list your property, create a host account and click on “Create a new listing.” Provide accurate details including property type, location, amenities, and high-quality photos. Set your pricing, availability, and house rules clearly. Once submitted, your listing will be reviewed and published for guests to book. Keep your calendar updated and respond promptly to inquiries to build trust and improve visibility.

Managing Bookings

As a host, you can manage bookings through your dashboard by viewing upcoming reservations, messaging guests, and adjusting availability. Use the calendar to block dates, set minimum stay requirements, or update pricing. Always review guest details and house rules before accepting requests. If changes arise, communicate clearly with guests and use the alteration or cancellation tools when necessary to maintain a smooth hosting experience.
