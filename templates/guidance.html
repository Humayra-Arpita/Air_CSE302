Travel Safety Tips

When traveling, always prioritize your safety by confirming the identity of your host and reviewing the property's details before booking. Communicate directly through the Airbnb platform to ensure your conversations remain secure. It's essential to share your travel plans with a trusted friend or family member. Always ensure the property is equipped with basic safety measures such as smoke detectors, fire extinguishers, and secure locks. During your stay, be aware of local safety guidelines and emergency contacts, and always trust your instincts in any situation.

Community Guidelines

At LavishBnB, we are committed to fostering a safe, respectful, and welcoming community for all. Our community guidelines are designed to ensure that both hosts and guests have a positive experience. We encourage respectful communication, kindness, and mutual understanding between everyone involved. Be mindful of your actions, respecting house rules, property, and your hosts or guests. Any behavior that is discriminatory, harmful, or inappropriate is strictly prohibited. Please report any concerns immediately so we can address them promptly, ensuring a safe environment for everyone.
